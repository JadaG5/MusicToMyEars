The Meaning of Music

Music is used for multiple purposes: entertainment, setting an environment, giving instruction, healing, etc. In every song, I feel like there is a message. It is something about certain pieces of music that makes me feel it on a deeper level. It is like the music is communicating directly with me. And what's better than listening to something I can relate to without having to actually say it myself?
